# Use Case: Prepare Coffee

## What is this about?

After an order is received, the coffee must be prepared. The process consists of several sub-steps such as grinding the beans, brewing the coffee, and optionally frothing milk.

## What is needed?

**Grinding:**

- Select coffee beans for the chosen beverage type
- Set the grind level
- Provide ground coffee powder

**Brewing:**

- Accept the ground coffee powder
- Select the appropriate brewing method
- Brew the coffee and make it ready

**Frothing milk:**

- Only execute if milk foam is desired
- Heat and froth the milk
- Add the milk foam to the coffee

## What must the system be able to do?

- Trigger the relevant preparation steps
- Skip optional steps when not needed
- Prepare the delivery after completion

## Why is this important?

Preparation is the central step of the workflow. It turns the order into a finished product that can be delivered.
